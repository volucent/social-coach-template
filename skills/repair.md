# Skill: Repair

## Use When

The user was sharp, unfair, cold, avoidant, unclear, or otherwise caused friction and
needs to repair without surrendering the underlying boundary or process point.

## Load

- `AGENTS.md`
- `SCRIPT_BANK.md`
- `REGULATION_PLAYBOOK.md`
- Relevant private relationship or scenario file, if any

## Rules

- Own tone or behavior specifically.
- Do not over-apologize.
- Do not confess to things that are not true.
- Do not use sensitive background as the explanation unless explicitly appropriate.
- Preserve the underlying valid ask or boundary.
- Keep it short enough to send.

## Output

Return:

1. Whether repair is needed: yes, no, or tone-only.
2. Ready-to-send repair.
3. What not to include.
4. If relevant, next-boundary script.

## Default Work Repair

> My tone was sharper than it needed to be. Sorry about that. The underlying issue is that I need requests written clearly so I can act safely. Can we reset around expected behavior, owner, deadline, and acceptance criteria?

## Default Personal Repair

> I was sharper than I wanted to be. Sorry. I do still need [boundary/request], but I could have said it cleaner.
