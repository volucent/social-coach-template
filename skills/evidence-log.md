# Skill: Evidence Log

## Use When

The user needs to convert a messy relationship incident into a neutral private
evidence entry for a recurring process problem.

## Load

- `AGENTS.md`
- `WORK_OPERATING_MANUAL.md`, for work situations
- Relevant private scenario notes, if any

## Rules

- This is not a vent document.
- Label evidence strength: direct, indirect, hearsay, or oral-only.
- Separate quote, pattern, impact, and escalation use.
- Avoid inflammatory labels.
- Do not overstate what the artifact proves.
- Include follow-up evidence to gather.
- Keep real evidence entries in `private/`.

## Output

Return a ready-to-paste private evidence entry with:

- Title.
- Evidence strength.
- People.
- Excerpt or summary.
- Pattern shown.
- Impact.
- Use in escalation.
- Follow-up evidence to gather.

## Evidence Strength Labels

- Direct: message, thread, ticket, doc, calendar event, or artifact exists.
- Indirect: someone directly involved described the event in writing.
- Hearsay: someone reported what another person experienced or said.
- Oral-only: event happened in conversation with no known written artifact yet.
