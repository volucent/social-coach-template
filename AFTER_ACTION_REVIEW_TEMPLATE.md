# After Action Review Template

Use after a charged work thread, social conflict, family event, or emotional blow-up. The
goal is learning, not self-punishment.

Copy this into a private dated note or private scenario file.

Date:

People:

## Observable Facts

- What was said or requested?
- What decision, deadline, or expectation existed?

## My Interpretation

- What did I believe it meant?
- What pattern did it remind me of?
- What did I assume about intent?

## Body / State

- Green, yellow, or red?
- Body cues:
- Urges:
- Did I want to flee, argue, prove, punish, explain, appease, or disappear?

## Action Taken

- What did I say or do?
- What did I not say or do?
- Did I delay, regulate, ask for facts, set a boundary, repair, or escalate?

## Outcome

- What happened next?
- Did the action protect credibility or the relationship?
- Did it move the situation toward a better operating model?
- Did it create cleanup work?

## Better Future Rule

- What should future-me do earlier next time?
- What script should be reused?
- What trigger should be added to `TRIGGER_MAP.md`?
- What evidence, if any, should be logged privately?

## Repair Needed?

- No repair needed.
- Repair needed for tone only.
- Repair needed for substance.
- Boundary needed instead of repair.

Draft repair if needed:

> My tone was sharper than it needed to be. Sorry about that. The underlying issue is [process / boundary / request]. Can we reset around [specific next step]?
