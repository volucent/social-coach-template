# Script Bank

Reusable scripts. Keep them short. No lies, no essays.

## Delay

> I need a little time to review this and avoid giving a rushed answer. I will follow up.

> I need to check the current facts before answering. I will respond once I have them in front of me.

> I do not want to answer from memory here. Please put the expected behavior and deadline in writing, and I will review.

## Work: Vague Request

> I cannot action this yet. Please add expected behavior, owner, deadline, and acceptance criteria.

> I can help once this is written as a clear request: desired behavior, affected users, deadline, business impact, acceptance criteria, and what happens if we do nothing.

## Work: Move To Shared Channel Or Ticket

> I want to keep this visible so nobody has to reconstruct context later. Please put the request in the shared channel or ticket, and I will respond there.

> Please move this into the shared channel so everyone has the same context.

## Work: Meeting With No Agenda

> Can you add the agenda and the decision needed from me? If there is no specific decision, I may skip and catch up from notes.

> I can attend if there is a decision to make. Please add the agenda and required outcome first.

## Work: Clean Blocker

> I am blocked until we have written expected behavior, acceptance criteria, and an owner for the decision.

> This request is mixing confirmation, new behavior, and timing urgency. Please split it into separate requests so we can evaluate safely.

## Work: Risk Or Approval Boundary

> This has risk attached. I need written approval and acceptance criteria before proceeding.

> I am not comfortable implementing this from verbal direction. Please put the decision in writing with the accountable owner included.

## Work: Repair After Sharp Tone

> My tone was sharper than it needed to be. Sorry about that. The underlying issue is that I need the request written clearly before I can act safely.

> I could have said that cleaner. My concern is still the process risk: we need the request written clearly before moving forward.

## Social: Clean No

> Can't make it, but thanks for thinking of me. Have fun.

> I am going to sit this one out, but I appreciate the invite.

## Social: Decline Format, Keep Person

> I am not up for a big group thing, but I would be down to hang one-on-one soon.

> That format is not really my thing, but I would genuinely like to see you. Want to do something quieter?

> Not feeling that plan, but I would be into [specific alternative]. Down?

## Social: Time-Boxed Yes

> I can come by from [time] to [time]. Looking forward to seeing you.

> I can stop by for a bit, but I have a hard stop at [time].

## Boundary

> I am not available for that.

> I am not discussing this tonight.

> I am happy to talk about this if we keep it concrete and calm. If it turns into blame, I am going to pause the conversation.

> I have already answered this. I am not going to keep relitigating it.

## Emotional Containment

> I can listen for 10 minutes, then I need us to drop it for tonight.

> I want to support you, but I cannot make this our whole evening.

## Friendship Maintenance

> I have been maxed out and not very social lately, but I do care about you. Want to do something low-key soon?

> I am bad at generic plans, but I would actually love to do something specific: [menu item].
